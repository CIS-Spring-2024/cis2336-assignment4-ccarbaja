Frequently Asked Questions

  • What allergens are in the kitchen?

  • Our kitchen (including our vegan menu items, although they do not contain dairy, eggs, or meat) is exposed to peanuts, lactose, eggs, fish, wheat, and soybeans.


  • Is the kitchen open on holidays?

  • The Honestly Amazing Cafeteria is not open for dinner on most holidays, including: Christmas, Thanksgiving, Independence Day, and Memorial Day.


  • Where can I buy UHSL merch?

  • UHSL merch is currently only sold in person due to limited quantities. We are working on attaching a shop to the website, but dates are still TBD.


  • Can I buy merch sold at events later?

  • We are thankful for all of our supporters who show up to pop up events. We want to give these supporters the opportunity to rock exclusive UHSL merch as a thank you, which means that some merch is exclusive/limited time only to specific events.
